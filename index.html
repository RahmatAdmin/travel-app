Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

What documents do I need for international travel?

You will need a valid passport, visa (if required), and other travel documents such as a travel itinerary and proof of accommodation. Check the specific requirements for your destination country.

How can I book a trip with your travel agency?

You can book a trip through our website by selecting your preferred package and completing the booking form. Alternatively, you can contact our customer support for assistance.

What is included in the travel packages?

Our travel packages typically include accommodation, transportation, guided tours, and some meals. The exact inclusions depend on the specific package you choose.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the package. Generally, you can cancel up to 14 days before departure for a full refund. Less than 14 days may incur a cancellation fee.
